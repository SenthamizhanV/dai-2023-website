About the Conference :

The third RBCDSAI Conference on Deployable AI organized by Robert Bosch Center for Data Science and AI (RBCDSAI), University of Helsinki, University of Oulu and the Center for Responsible AI, IIT Madras, will take place in hybrid mode from June 05 to 07, 2023. The sub themes for this edition are Edge AI and Responsible AI.

During the first two days, there will be keynotes and invited talks on each sub theme. The last day of the program will have spotlight talks from students and a poster viewing cum mentoring session. The conference is useful for students and early stage researchers for getting peer review and feedback for their research work and scientific papers. Also the industrial AI experts interested in the themes are encouraged to participate and connect with researchers.
